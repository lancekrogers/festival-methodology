## Usage Guide

This FESTIVAL_GOAL.md file should be:

1. Created at festival inception
2. Placed at the festival root directory
3. Referenced by all phase goals
4. Updated throughout the festival lifecycle
5. Completed with thorough evaluation after festival completion

The goal serves as:

- North star for entire festival
- Alignment tool for all participants
- Success measurement framework
- Stakeholder communication document
- Organizational learning capture

### Example (Filled Out)

# Festival Goal: E-Commerce Platform MVP

**Festival:** E-Commerce Platform MVP | **Status:** Active | **Created:** 2024-01-01 | **Target Completion:** 2024-03-01

## Festival Objective

**Primary Goal:** Build a fully functional e-commerce platform MVP with product catalog, shopping cart, checkout, and order management capabilities.

**Vision Statement:** Create a modern, scalable e-commerce platform that enables small businesses to sell products online with minimal setup. The platform should handle 1000 concurrent users, process payments securely, and provide a delightful shopping experience that converts visitors into customers.

## Success Criteria

The festival goal is achieved when:

### Functional Success

- [ ] **Product Management**: Admin can create, update, delete products with images
- [ ] **Shopping Experience**: Users can browse, search, and filter products
- [ ] **Purchase Flow**: Complete checkout with payment processing works
- [ ] **Order Management**: Users can track orders, admins can manage fulfillment

### Quality Success

- [ ] **Performance**: Page load time < 2 seconds at 95th percentile
- [ ] **Availability**: 99.9% uptime during business hours
- [ ] **Security**: Passes PCI compliance scan for payment processing

### Business Success

- [ ] **Launch Ready**: Platform can accept real customer orders
- [ ] **Scalability**: Architecture supports 10x growth without major rework
- [ ] **Cost Effective**: Monthly infrastructure cost < $500 at launch scale

### User Success

- [ ] **Conversion**: Test users complete purchase in < 5 minutes
- [ ] **Mobile Experience**: 100% of features work on mobile devices
- [ ] **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance achieved
